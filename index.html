Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our programs

What subjects do you offer for coaching?

+

We offer comprehensive coaching in Mathematics, Science, English, and Social Studies for students from Class 9 to 12. Our curriculum covers both CBSE and ICSE boards with specialized attention to each subject's requirements.

How do your live classes work?

+

Our live classes are conducted through our advanced virtual classroom platform. Students can interact with instructors in real-time, ask questions, participate in discussions, and access recorded sessions for revision. Classes are scheduled at convenient times and include interactive whiteboards, screen sharing, and breakout rooms.

What makes your coaching different from others?

+

Our coaching stands out through personalized learning plans, experienced faculty, proven track record of success (with multiple toppers), small batch sizes for individual attention, comprehensive study materials, regular assessments, and 24/7 doubt resolution support. We focus on conceptual understanding rather than rote learning.

Do you provide study materials and practice tests?

+

Yes, we provide comprehensive study materials including detailed notes, practice worksheets, previous year question papers, mock tests, and video lectures. Our practice tests are designed to simulate actual exam conditions and help students improve their time management and problem-solving skills.

How can I track my child's progress?

+

We provide detailed progress reports through our student portal. Parents can access regular performance analytics, attendance records, test scores, and improvement areas. We also conduct parent-teacher meetings and provide personalized feedback on student performance and areas for improvement.

What is your success rate and track record?

+

We have an excellent track record with multiple students achieving top ranks in their respective boards. Our recent achievements include city toppers, district toppers, and school toppers across various subjects. We maintain a success rate of over 95% with students scoring above 90% in their board examinations.

How do I enroll my child in your programs?

+

Enrollment is simple! You can register online through our website, call us for assistance, or visit our center. We offer a free demo class to help you understand our teaching methodology. After enrollment, students receive access to our learning platform and can start attending classes immediately.

Do you offer any scholarships or discounts?

+

Yes, we offer various scholarship programs based on academic merit, early bird discounts, sibling discounts, and special packages for multiple subjects. We also have need-based scholarships for deserving students. Contact our admission team for detailed information about current offers and eligibility criteria.
